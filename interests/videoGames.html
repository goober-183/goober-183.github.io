I like Video Games, but which ones do I like? Here's a List in no particular Order

Team Fortress 2

I like TF2, it's a very deep game mechanic wise while being easy to pickup and play (i main soilder and heavy.)
Now that the bots are gone, harvest queue is really fun tbh.

Ratchet and Clank (series)

I love The gameplay of ratchet and clank, so i like all of the games (except for the PSP ones) but here are my
top 3 of all 15(?) of em! (and in order) (having a million goofy weapons accecible at any time is fun af)

Ratchet And Clank: Up Your Arsenal (3)

This is Definitly where the series peaked, The soundtrack aint terrible and the weapons are fun to use. The story
is also really funny and the multiplayer is fun as hell

Ratchet and Clank (2002)

This is the game that started the whole series (so expect some growing pains) but the game is hella fun to mess
around with if you know the movement tech. With a killer soundtrack to boot!

Ratchet And Clank: A Crack In Time

the story is good, and for the first time, I enjoyed the space sections. Also time travel is cool (i like back to
the future :D).

Half-Life 2

Like this one for the same reason i like the Ratchet games, (having a million goofy weapons accecible at any time
is fun af). Also the soundtrack gets good at times, and hitting those revolver shots is satisfying.
